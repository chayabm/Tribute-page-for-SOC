Steven Universe

Steven universe is the titular character of the Cartoon Network TV show 'Steven Universe'. He is a member of the ‘Crystal Gems’ – a group of gems (magical beings from space) who protect the Earth from attacks. As the first ever human-gem hybrid, we follow Steven’s journey as he discovers himself in learning about his identity, his emotions, and his relationships.

Here are some facts about Steven:

  • He is 13 years old at the start of season 1
  • He has a pet lion with teleporting abilities
  • He can produce a pink shield using his gem
